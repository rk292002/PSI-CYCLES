ABOUT


Bicycle-sharing systems have spurt up all over the globe especially since smartphones became commonplace. In India, several such services are operating in various communities and Ari is proud to join the ranks. Ari inherits some aspects from one of the most successful models, NammaCycle, working in IISC Bangalore. 4th Gen cycle-sharing is a micro-mobility system that involves the dynamic distributed use of cycles that can be picked and dropped off at any location within a designated area.
